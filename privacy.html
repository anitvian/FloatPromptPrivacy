Privacy Policy for FloatPrompt

Last modified: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

FloatPrompt respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection

  • We do not collect personal data on our servers.
  • Scripts are stored locally on your device.
  • Video recordings are saved locally to your device's Gallery.

3. Permissions

  • Helper/Overlay: Used to display the script over other apps.
  • Camera/Microphone: Used only when you actively record a video.
  • MediaProjection: Used for screen recording features (optional).

4. Advertising

We use AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data for personalized advertising.

5. Contact

For questions, contact: floatprompt@gmail.com
